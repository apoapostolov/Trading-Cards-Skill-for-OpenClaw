# Panini Novelty & Special Card Types (2023–2026)

Comprehensive reference of all novelty, parallel, insert, and special card types produced by Panini across basketball, football, and other sports from 2023 through early 2026.

> **Sources:** Beckett, Cardboard Connection, Checklist Insider, Sports Collectors Daily, Panini America blog, SI Collectibles. Data current as of March 2026.

## 4. Obsidian

- **Name:** Obsidian
- **Description:** Dark card stock with colorful imagery and electric etch-style parallels. Sleek, premium look.
- **Parallels:** Hyper, Pulsar, Ruby Wave, Silver, Snakeskin, plus numbered Red /299, Skewed /249, Basketball /225, Blue /199, White /175, Purple Ice /149, Blue Ice /125, and higher rarity parallels.
- **Insert:** Lucky Envelopes (insert set with scratch-off redemption style)
- **Product:** Panini Obsidian Basketball, Obsidian Football
- **Years:** 2023–2026

## 7. Spectra

- **Name:** Spectra
- **Description:** Chromium cards with vibrant colors and holographic patterns. Known for Eye Candy and Die-Cut parallels.
- **Key Parallels:** Silver, Holo Gold, Purple, Neon Green, Black (1/1), and various die-cut variants
- **Product:** Panini Spectra Basketball, Spectra Football
- **Years:** 2023–2026

---

## 8. Court Kings

- **Name:** Court Kings
- **Description:** Art-style cards with unique illustrative designs. Features both standard and premium configurations.
- **Notable Types:**
  - **Acetate Rookies** — clear acetate stock rookie cards (International Blaster exclusive)
  - **Autograph cards** — various signed insert sets
  - **Memorabilia cards** — jersey/patch cards in Hobby boxes
- **Product:** Panini Court Kings Basketball
- **Years:** 2023–2026

---

## 9. Silhouette

- **Name:** Silhouette
- **Description:** Brand-new product (debut 2024-25). Features silhouette-style player imagery with possible die-cut and acetate inserts.
- **Product:** Panini Silhouette Basketball
- **Years:** 2025–2026

## 10. Contenders & Contenders Optic

### 10.1 Contenders (Football/Basketball)
- **Name:** Contenders
- **Description:** Draft-ticket style cards. The iconic "Rookie Ticket" autograph is the flagship chase.
- **Key Types:**
  - **Rookie Ticket Autographs** — on-card autos in draft ticket format
  - **Ticket Stub parallels** — vari-colored ticket stub variants
  - **Championship Ticket** — championship-themed subset
- **Years:** 2023–2026

### 10.2 Contenders Optic
- **Name:** Contenders Optic
- **Description:** Chromium version of Contenders with Opti-chrome parallels.
- **Key Types:** Rookie Ticket Optic Autos, Cracked Ice parallels, various numbered parallels
- **Years:** 2023–2026

---

## 24. Redemption Cards

- **Name:** Redemption Cards
- **Description:** Cards exchanged for the actual hit at a later date. Used when autos or patches aren't ready at production time.
- **Products:** Found across most Panini products when timing requires
- **Years:** 2023–2026 (increasingly phased out in favor of on-card/live production)

## 29. Key Rarity Tiers (Summary)

From most common to rarest across Panini products:

1. **Base** — Unnumbered, standard print
2. **Base Parallels** — Silver, Snakeskin, Hyper, Ice, Wave patterns (unnumbered)
3. **Short Prints (SP)** — Prizmania, Groovy, Choice Peacock
4. **Super Short Prints (SSP)** — Colorblast, certain insert variations
5. **Numbered Commons** — Red /299, Blue /199, Purple /99
6. **Numbered Rares** — Mojo /25, Gold /10, Green /5
7. **Ultra Rares** — Gold Snakeskin /8, Nebula 1/1, Black 1/1
8. **1/1 Masterpieces** — Black Prizms, Nebula, Logoman, Triple Logoman, Diamond Trophy
